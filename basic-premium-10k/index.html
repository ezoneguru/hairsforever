Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How long does a premium hair patch last? +
With proper care and regular maintenance, our premium hair patches typically last 12-18 months. The luxury tier with European hair can last even longer with our lifetime support and maintenance program.
Is the hair patch completely undetectable? +
Absolutely! Our hair patches are designed using advanced techniques to look 100% natural. We perfectly match color, texture, density, and hairline to your natural hair. Even from close inspection, they're virtually undetectable.
Can I swim, exercise, and lead an active lifestyle? +
Yes! Our hair patches are designed for active lifestyles. You can swim, exercise, play sports, and engage in any activity. We use advanced attachment methods that ensure your hair patch stays secure during all activities.
What maintenance is required? +
We recommend professional maintenance every 4-6 weeks. This includes cleaning, reattachment, styling, and general upkeep. We provide detailed at-home care instructions and lifetime support to all our clients.
Do you offer EMI or payment plans? +
Yes! We offer flexible EMI options starting at just ₹2,999/month. We work with leading banks and NBFCs to provide 0% interest EMI plans. Speak to our consultants for personalized payment solutions.
What is your money-back guarantee policy? +
We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee on our Premium and Luxury packages. If you're not completely happy within the first 30 days, we'll either remake your hair patch free of charge or provide a full refund. Your satisfaction is our priority.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How long does a hair patch last? +
With proper care and maintenance, our premium hair patches typically last 9-12 months. The longevity depends on factors like hair quality, maintenance routine, and usage patterns.
Is the hair patch detectable? +
No! Our hair patches are designed to look completely natural. We match the color, texture, and density to your natural hair, ensuring seamless blending. Even from close up, they're virtually undetectable.
Can I swim or exercise with a hair patch? +
Yes! Our hair patches are designed for an active lifestyle. You can swim, exercise, and engage in sports. We use advanced attachment methods that ensure your hair patch stays secure during all activities.
How often do I need maintenance? +
We recommend maintenance every 4-6 weeks to ensure your hair patch continues to look its best. This includes cleaning, reattachment if needed, and general upkeep.
What is the cost range? +
Our hair patches range from ₹15,000 to ₹75,000 depending on the size, hair quality, and customization level. We also offer flexible EMI options to make it affordable. Book a free consultation for an accurate quote.
Do you offer a warranty or guarantee? +
Yes! We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely happy with your hair patch within the first 30 days, we'll remake it free of charge or provide a full refund.
